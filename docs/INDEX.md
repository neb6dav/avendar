# Avendar: The Crucible of Legends — Documentation Index

This directory contains scraped and compiled documentation from [avendar.net](https://avendar.net), covering both the game's lore and mechanics.

Avendar: The Crucible of Legends is a text-based Multi-User Dungeon (MUD) founded in 1998, set in a fully original high fantasy world. Connect at `avendar.net` port `9999`.

## Quick Reference

### The Three Overgods
- **Iandir** — Neutral / Creation / Natural Law
- **Jolinn** — Good / Life / the Sea
- **Ashur (Tzet-Askhari)** — Evil / Death / Destruction / Void

### The Ten Playable Races
Alatharya, Aelin, Caladaran, Ch'taren, Chaja, Ethron, Human, Kankoran, Nefortu, Shuddeni, Srryn

### The Six Elemental Spheres
Air · Earth · Fire · Water · Spirit · Void

### The Major Historical Events
1. **The Age of Creation** — Overgods fashion the world; the alatharya are created
2. **The Sundering** — Alatharya's hubris punished; magic fragmented into six spheres
3. **The War of Fire** — Srryn, humans, and nefortu burn the world; aelin civilization devastated
4. **The War of Night** — Shuddeni invade; ch'taren arrive; Serachel defeated
5. **The Modern Era** — Independent city-states; Great Houses fight for the Stones of Power

### The Great Houses
- **Champions of Avendar** (Good)
- **Guardians of the Law** (Lawful)
- **Raiders of Twilight** (Neutral/Variable)
- **The Shunned** (Evil, Serachel's cult)
- **Followers of Dolgrael** (Honor-bound)
